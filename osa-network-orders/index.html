▸ Document Overview

The "Leaked OSA Network Orders.pdf" outlines the operational guidelines for the Office of Special Affairs (OSA) within the Sea Organization, a religious order in Scientology. These orders are essentially a manual for engaging in what is described as a "war" against perceived enemies of Scientology, with the ultimate goal of "ESTABLISHING THE INDISPENSABILITY OF SCIENTOLOGY".

PRIMARY PURPOSE: "ESTABLISHING THE INDISPENSABILITY OF SCIENTOLOGY" - positioning the organization as essential to society's survival, believing "No other movement can handle psychosomatics, drugs, education, crime, insanity and a long list of social ills."

The Perceived Enemy

The orders extensively detail Scientology's "enemies," primarily identified as:

• Psychiatry and Mental Health Organizations: The World Federation of Mental Health (WFMH) and National Association of Mental Health (NAMH) are portrayed as "madmen" responsible for "murder, torture, and brain damage of tens of thousands of people."
