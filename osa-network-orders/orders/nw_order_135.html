BOOK DISCLAIMERS

(Excerpted from an LRH evaluation on a legal case in 1973.)

Compose, with Legal assistance, a disclaimer that does not invalidate the work but explains that the Church, while it is perfectly at liberty to so do, does not engage in psychosomatic handling or accept persons for treatment of disease of insanity and offers spiritual and religious counseling; that the attainment of betterment is but the responsibility of the person himself; that the E-Meter does nothing by itself and serves only as a guide to ministers of the Church; that the author was recounting only observations as a consequence of his work and is not responsible for any promises or misuse of this material; etc., with import to be that the person reads these works as his own responsibility. The purpose of this design is handsomely like a book plate with space for the person's name on it.
This book belongs to:

All countries.

L. RON HUBBARD
